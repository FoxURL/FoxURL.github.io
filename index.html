How it works (kid-friendly):
  • You paste a big link.
  • We make a tiny code, like fox12.
  • We save code → big link in your browser only.
  • Your share link looks like thispage#go=fox12.
  • When someone opens it, their browser jumps to the big link.

Note: Because this is all in your browser, if you clear site data, your saved codes here will disappear.
